📋 Before You Park
  • Always ask permission at private lots before staying
  • Park in well-lit areas near other vehicles
  • Call 211 — they know unlisted safe parking programs
  • Have a backup spot planned before you need it
⚠️ While Sleeping
  • Keep phone charged — 911 works without cell service
  • Never run engine for heat — carbon monoxide risk
  • Keep doors locked and trust your instincts
⚖️ Your Rights
  • Police cannot search your vehicle without consent or a warrant
  • You don't have to say where you live
  • Free legal guides: nlchp.org/vehicle-residency
