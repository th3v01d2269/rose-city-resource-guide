Generally Overnight-Friendly
⚠️ Always ask permission first. Policies vary by individual location. Be respectful and leave no trace.
📋 Before You Park
  • Always ask permission at private lots before staying overnight
  • Park in well-lit areas near other vehicles when possible
  • Some cities ban vehicle habitation — know local ordinances
  • Have a backup spot planned before you need it
  • Call 211 — they know unlisted local safe parking programs
⚠️ While Sleeping
  • Use window covers for privacy and temperature regulation
  • Keep phone charged — 911 works without cell service
  • Never run engine for heat — carbon monoxide risk
  • Keep doors locked and trust your instincts
⚖️ Your Rights
  • Police cannot search your vehicle without consent or a warrant
  • You don't have to say where you live or what you're doing
  • National Homelessness Law Center: nlchp.org — free legal guides
  • Document badge numbers and car numbers if harassed
