Generally Overnight-Friendly Spots
⚠️ Always ask permission first. Individual locations vary. Be respectful and leave no trace.
📋 Before You Park
  • Always ask permission at private lots
  • Park in well-lit areas near other vehicles
  • Know local ordinances — some cities ban vehicle habitation
  • Have a backup spot ready in case you're asked to leave
  • Call 211 — they know unlisted safe parking programs
  • Keep registration and ID accessible
⚠️ While Sleeping
  • Use window covers for privacy and temperature
  • Keep phone charged — 911 works without cell service
  • Never run engine for heat — carbon monoxide risk
  • Keep doors locked and trust your instincts
  • Emergency kit: water, food, flashlight, first aid, documents
⚖️ Your Rights
  • Police cannot search your vehicle without consent or a warrant
  • You don't have to say where you live
  • Document badge numbers if harassed
  • National Homelessness Law Center: nlchp.org — free legal guides
  • Many cities have safe parking ordinances protecting vehicle dwellers
