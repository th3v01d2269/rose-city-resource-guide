Generally Overnight-Friendly Spots
⚠️ Always ask permission first. Individual locations vary. Be respectful, leave no trace, and move on if asked.
📋 Before You Park
  • Always ask permission at private lots before staying overnight
  • Park in well-lit areas near other vehicles when possible
  • Know local laws — some cities have vehicle habitation bans
  • Have a backup spot planned before you need it
  • Call 211 — they know unlisted local safe parking programs
  • Keep your registration and ID accessible
⚠️ While Sleeping
  • Use window covers for privacy and temperature regulation
  • Keep phone charged — 911 works even without cell service
  • Never run the engine for heat — carbon monoxide risk
  • Keep doors locked and trust your instincts
  • Carbon monoxide detector in vehicle is strongly recommended
⚖️ Your Rights
  • Police cannot search your vehicle without consent or a warrant
  • You don't have to tell officers where you live or what you're doing
  • Document badge numbers and patrol car numbers if harassed
  • Free legal help: National Homelessness Law Center — nlchp.org
  • Many cities have ordinances specifically protecting vehicle dwellers
